Current Focus

No cause. No cure. No map.

  • Coarctation of the aorta is repaired surgically every day, yet the cause is unknown.
  • Every theory skips one step: no one has mapped how the normal ductal-aortic junction develops across gestation.
  • I am building that missing baseline, so competing theories can become testable mechanism.
